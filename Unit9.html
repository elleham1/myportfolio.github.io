UNIT 9 Spreadsheet Development

Unit 9 focuses mainly on using excel spreadsheet to help Coventry football club take data of season ticket holder information. This unit contains two assignments, in assignments one we were assigned to identify the uses, features and functions of a spreadsheet. Example of these functions include sum, average, minimum, maximum and count. Furthermore, reviewing how its feature would help improve productivity and accuracy whiles highlighting its strength and weaknesses, click to view Assignment 1.

In assignment two, we used excel spreadsheet and Microsoft word to produce a general prototype of how data would be taken if a customer purchases a ticket. The excel spreadsheet holds information such as supporters name, house address, contact number and season ticket type. Assignment two done using Microsoft word contain three task, task one was to produce a design for our spreadsheet how the output data will be presented and the user requirement that comes with it, click to view Assignment 2.
